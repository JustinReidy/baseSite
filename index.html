About Me

I started my dive into programming when I was 11 or 12, when I discovered writing batch programs. I was instantly hooked, and from there began working on expanding my knowledge. Moving into web development seemed to be a logical step for myself, until I found myself only wanting to work on web based projects. My main focus when developing sites for clients is mobile responsiveness and a mobile first development ideology.

SEO and Mobile Responsiveness

SEO and Mobile "friendliness go hand in hand, in April, 2015 Google began taking mobile responsiveness into account when displaying their search results.
A study by the PEW institute showed that more people in the US have a smartphone (81%) than a desktop/laptop computer (74%)
